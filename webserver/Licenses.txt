The Dana wrapper source code for these libraries is available at http://www.projectdana.com/source/ and your (direct or indirect) re-use of these libraries must be in accordance with the terms of their individual licenses. You should also note that Dana is capable of downloading additional native libraries using the source package management system; it is your responsibility to understand any license implications of any such additiona libraries beyond those listed below.
